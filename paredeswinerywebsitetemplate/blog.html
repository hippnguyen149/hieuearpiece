This IEM seems to be unknown at Head-fi, so I decided to do a review. Annoyingly, I'm not sure what the sonic differences are between the Bmaster, Bmaster PRO, and the newest "Bmasters MMCX" with more interesting colors and smoother shell. It looks like the "Bmaster MMCX" has a better MMCX cable than the original Bmaster, and the Bmaster PRO appears to have better bass, isolation, and extra tips. Bmaster = 3 sets of tips; Bmaster MMCX & Bmaster PRO = 4 sets of tips. Each model appears to have 2x 10mm DD drivers and 1x BA driver, but looking inside the IEM I see what appears to be 1x DD and 1x BA, unless the DD's are stacked on top of each other. These IEMs definitely need more power than usual, nearly as much power as 7Hertz Timeless.
